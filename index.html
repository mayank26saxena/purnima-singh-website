Purnima Singh

Institute Chair Professor (Psychology), IIT Delhi
New Delhi, India

Prof. Purnima Singh is a professor at the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences at IIT Delhi. Her research areas include: Group Processes, Justice, Identity, and Intergroup Relations in Organizational and Social contexts.
